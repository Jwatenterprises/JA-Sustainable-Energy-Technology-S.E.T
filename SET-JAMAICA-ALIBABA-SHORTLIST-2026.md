## ⚡ Technical Requirements for Jamaica

To ensure the equipment works with Jamaica's electrical grid (JPS), you **MUST** specify these in every RFQ (Request for Quote):

1.  **Inverter Phase:** "Split-Phase 110V/220V" or "Split-Phase 120V/240V, 60Hz".
2.  **Battery Voltage:** "51.2V LiFePO4" (Standard 16-cell configuration).
3.  **Communication:** "Inverter-Battery BMS Communication Protocol" (Ensure the inverter and battery can "talk" via CAN/RS485).
4.  **Certifications:** "IEC 62109, UL 1741 (for inverters), UN38.3 (for battery shipping)".

## 🚢 Shipping & Customs Strategy

*   **Preferred Incoterm:** **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid).**
    *   The supplier handles freight, Jamaican customs, GCT, and delivery to your Kingston address. This is the only "hassle-free" way to source.
*   **Wharfage Warning:** If you choose **CIF**, you will be hit with 15% GCT + Duties + Customs fees + Wharfage at the Kingston port. This usually adds 40%+ to the cost.
*   **Target Landed Cost (2026 Benchmark):**
    *   10kW Hybrid Inverter + 15kWh Battery: **~$2,500 - $3,000 USD (DDP to Kingston).**
