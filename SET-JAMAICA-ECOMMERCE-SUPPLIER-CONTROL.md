## Jamaica Customs Notes

Verified against Jamaica Customs Agency pages on 2026-05-30:

- Solar panels and solar inverters: Import Duty 0%, GCT exempt using ANC EC3.
- SCF, Environmental Levy, and CAF still apply.
- De minimis ecommerce rule: if FOB value is US$100 or less, duties and taxes are not charged.
- If FOB value exceeds US$100, duties and taxes are charged on the full CIF value.

Use these as pricing-planning rules only. Final landed cost must still be verified by the supplier, courier/freight forwarder, or customs broker before binding quotes.

Sources:
- Jamaica Customs Agency: `https://jca.gov.jm/faq/what-are-the-duties-applicable-to-solar-panels-and-solar-inverters/`
- Jamaica Customs Agency: `https://jca.gov.jm/individual/ecommerce/de-minimis/`

## Pricing Lanes

### Low-Ticket Dropship Products

Target FOB: US$100 or less where practical.

Product categories:
- Solar security lights and motion lights
- LED bulbs
- Small power banks
- Energy monitors
- Outdoor security lights
- Emergency solar lanterns
- Portable rechargeable fans
- Solar panel cleaning kits and accessories

Rules:
- Confirm Jamaica shipping before listing.
- Prefer low-voltage, low-risk, easy-to-replace products.
- Require supplier warranty and return process.
- Use Syncee first for cleaner WooCommerce sync; use CJdropshipping only after sample testing.

### High-Ticket Solar Products

Products:
- Small solar generators / backup power stations
- Complete quote-based 3 kW, 5 kW, and 10 kW solar kits
- Hybrid inverters
- LiFePO4 battery bundles
- Panels, mounting hardware, charge controllers, and full kits

Rules:
- Quote-based sale only.
- Require freight quote, customs estimate, datasheets, warranty, certifications, and professional installation consultation.
- Use Alibaba.com for RFQs, supplier chat, Trade Assurance quotes, and sample/procurement workflow.
- Do not allow automatic checkout for high-ticket or safety-critical electrical products until supplier, customs, and installation paths are locked.
