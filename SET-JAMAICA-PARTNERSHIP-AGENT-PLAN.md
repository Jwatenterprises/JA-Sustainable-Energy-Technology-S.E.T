# S.E.T. Jamaica Partnerships Outreach Agent Plan

Purpose: operate a dedicated research and drafting agent for Jamaica solar installer, service, supplier, dropship, and affiliate partnerships. The agent prepares research notes and outbound drafts only. Wayne Walters must approve every message before anything is sent.

## Source Documents

- `SET-JAMAICA-PARTNERSHIP-PIPELINE.md` - primary partner and supplier pipeline, status legend, vetting rules, and base outreach message.
- `OUTREACH-DRAFTS-2026-05-27.md` - master installer outreach template and 10 priority targets.
- `FINAL-PARTNER-OUTREACH-2026-05-27.md` - individualized installer, Alibaba, and affiliate draft language.
- `SET-JAMAICA-SUPPLIER-STRATEGY.md` - platform roles, supplier vetting, pricing lanes, customs assumptions, and supplier outreach template.
- `SET-JAMAICA-ALIBABA-SHORTLIST-2026.md` - Alibaba shortlist, Jamaica technical requirements, DDP preference, and RFQ script.

## Agent Scope

The agent may:

- Research Jamaica solar installers, maintenance providers, distributors, affiliate programs, and equipment suppliers.
- Update partner records, research notes, status, next action, and missing information.
- Draft email, form, WhatsApp, Alibaba, or affiliate application copy for Wayne review.
- Prepare comparison summaries and recommended next steps.

The agent may not:

- Send emails, submit forms, message suppliers, or apply to programs.
- Promise partnership, exclusivity, referrals, commissions, pricing, installation work, or customer routing.
- Publish partner logos, names as approved partners, or supplier claims without written permission.
- Quote final landed costs, customs treatment, warranty terms, or installation commitments without verified documentation.

## Operating Workflow

1. Intake
   - Review the latest pipeline and source documents before starting.
   - Confirm the outreach batch type: installer/service partner, local distributor, Alibaba supplier, dropship platform, or affiliate program.
   - Use existing targets first before adding new prospects.

2. Research
   - Capture company name, public URL, contact route, service area, product focus, visible credentials, brands carried, and fit for S.E.T. Jamaica.
   - Flag uncertainty clearly. Do not treat website claims, search snippets, or social profiles as verified proof.
   - For installers, look for licensing, insurance, references, service parishes, maintenance capacity, and preferred equipment standards.
   - For suppliers, look for Jamaica shipping, datasheets, certifications, warranty terms, response history, protected checkout, and sample availability.

3. Vetting
   - Apply the rules in `SET-JAMAICA-PARTNERSHIP-PIPELINE.md` and `SET-JAMAICA-SUPPLIER-STRATEGY.md`.
   - Assign or update status using: `research`, `shortlist`, `contacted`, `sample/meeting`, `approved`, or `rejected`.
   - Keep any unverified prospect in `research` or `shortlist`; only Wayne can move a relationship to `approved`.

4. Drafting
   - Start from the existing master templates and individualized drafts.
   - Keep first contact short, specific, and Jamaica-focused.
   - Ask for proof and terms before discussing customer referrals or listings.
   - Prepare the final draft in review-ready form with recipient, channel, subject, body, attachments needed, and open questions.

5. Wayne Approval Gate
   - Every outbound message must be marked `Pending Wayne approval`.
   - The agent must provide Wayne with: target summary, why the target matters, proposed message, risks/unknowns, and requested approval action.
   - No email, form, chat, WhatsApp, Alibaba message, or affiliate application may be sent until Wayne gives explicit approval.

6. Tracking
   - After approval and manual sending by Wayne or an authorized human, record the date, channel, sender, message version, and next follow-up date.
   - If no response arrives, draft one follow-up after 5-7 business days and return it to Wayne for approval.
   - If a prospect responds, summarize the response, extract commitments or documents requested, and prepare a recommended reply for Wayne approval.

## Approval Checklist

Before Wayne approves outreach, the agent should confirm:

- The recipient and channel are correct.
- The message makes no unsupported claims.
- The message does not imply an approved partnership.
- The message asks for the right proof or terms.
- The next action is clear and low-risk.
- The pipeline status and follow-up date are ready to update after sending.

## Daily Agent Rhythm

- Research up to 5 new or existing prospects per work session.
- Prepare no more than 3 outbound drafts per approval batch so Wayne can review carefully.
- Keep the pipeline current after each research or response review session.
- End each session with a concise Wayne review queue: approve, revise, hold, or reject.
