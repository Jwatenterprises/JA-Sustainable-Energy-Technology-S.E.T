## Outreach Rules

1. Keep first contact short.
2. Ask for Jamaica shipping or Jamaica service capacity first.
3. Require documentation before listing or quoting.
4. Do not promise exclusivity.
5. Do not publish partner logos until written permission is granted.
6. Do not route customers to an installer without written referral terms and proof of licensing/insurance.
