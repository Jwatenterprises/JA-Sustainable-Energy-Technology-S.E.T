# S.E.T. Jamaica Supplier Strategy

## Platform Roles

### Alibaba.com
Best overall fit for the serious solar side of S.E.T. Jamaica.

Use for:
- Solar panels
- Inverters
- LiFePO4 batteries
- Hybrid systems
- Charge controllers
- Mounting hardware
- Complete solar kits

Operating rule:
- Use Alibaba for wholesale, RFQs, samples, Trade Assurance orders, and quote-based solar system sourcing.
- Do not list high-ticket or safety-critical electrical items for automatic checkout until supplier, certification, shipping, warranty, and installation path are verified.

### Syncee
Best fit for WooCommerce dropshipping.

Use for:
- Consumer-friendly energy-saving products
- Smaller solar accessories
- Cleaner online store product sync
- Automated product and order sync
- Vetted marketplace suppliers

Operating rule:
- Use Syncee for low-ticket add-ons that can be sold online without professional installation.

### CJdropshipping
Best fit for testing trending products.

Use for:
- Low-risk product tests
- Small consumer accessories
- Trend validation before committing to inventory

Operating rule:
- Require samples first.
- Avoid high-ticket or safety-critical electrical items until tested.

## Jamaica Customs Working Assumptions

These points came from project research and must be verified directly before being used in public copy, checkout logic, or binding quotes:

- Solar panels and solar inverters may qualify for 0% import duty and GCT exemption using ANC EC3.
- SCF, Environmental Levy, and CAF may still apply.
- Ordinary ecommerce goods with FOB value US$100 or less may not be charged duties/taxes.
- If FOB value exceeds US$100, duties/taxes may be charged on the full CIF value.

Action:
- Verify with Jamaica Customs or a licensed customs broker before publishing or quoting final landed-cost estimates.

## Supplier Vetting Rules

Minimum requirements before adding products:

| Vetting item | Minimum requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Seller rating | 4.6/5 or higher where available |
| Order history | 500+ orders or proven B2B transaction history |
| Communication | 90%+ response rate or reply within 24 hours |
| Shipping to Jamaica | Confirmed before listing |
| Product proof | Datasheet, warranty, certification, and sample order |
| Solar/electrical products | CE, UL, IEC, or relevant specs where applicable |
| Payment protection | Alibaba Trade Assurance or platform-protected checkout |
| Returns | Written return/refund policy before launch |
