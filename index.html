The Jamaican Financial Advantage

Every proposal should show the full landed-cost math.

Solar decisions in Jamaica depend on system design, shipping, customs treatment, available incentives, financing, and installation quality. S.E.T. separates the estimate from the final quote so the numbers stay defensible.

%

30% Income Tax Credit

Residential and SME customers can claim a 30% income tax credit on total PV system costs (capped at J$1.2M). Unused credits can be carried forward.

0%

GCT & Import Duty Exempt

Solar panels, inverters, and Li-ion batteries are 0% Duty and GCT-Exempt under Additional National Code (ANC) EC3. Minor SCF/ENVL/CAF fees still apply.

📊

Accelerated Capital Allowance

Commercial clients (SMEs) can write off system costs quickly. We provide the documentation required for your accountant to claim these incentives.
